What value this brings
A way for AI products to stand out
The AI app space looks increasingly the same. Treating motion and light as structural material — not decoration — gives an emerging product a distinct, ownable feel.
Delight as differentiation
When core capabilities converge, the interaction layer becomes the moat. Felt state — energy flowing, dispersing, converging — makes a product memorable.
A coherent system, not effects
Because every motion is a token, delight stays consistent across the whole product instead of becoming one-off flourishes that drift over time.
